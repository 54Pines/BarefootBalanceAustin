About Us

Alena Pavlow is a licensed massage therapist from Houston Texas. She moved to Austin over four years ago to pursue her passion of healing. She has been practicing for over seven years now, focusing her career on learning and perfecting her deep tissue skills. Adding Ashiatsu was just another way to help her clients further. Working mostly in spas, she also gained some "ultimate relaxation" practice. In Austin she has worked for some of the top facilities, but now enjoys using her time to build her business along with others.
